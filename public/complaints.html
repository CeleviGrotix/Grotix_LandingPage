Complaints Book

In accordance with Peruvian consumer protection regulations, Grotix — operated by Celevi S.A.C. — provides this Complaints Book so that users may formally register any complaint or claim regarding our products and services. We are committed to addressing every submission within the legally mandated timeframe.

01

What Is This

The Complaints Book (Libro de Reclamaciones) is a mechanism required by Peruvian law (D.S. 011-2011-PCM and the Consumer Protection Code, Law No. 29571) through which any consumer may formally register a complaint or claim against a supplier of goods or services.

A complaint refers to dissatisfaction with the service or product that is not related to a legal infringement. A claim refers to dissatisfaction directly associated with a product or service defect, non-compliance, or consumer rights violation.

Submitting a complaint or claim through this book does not limit your right to seek other legal remedies available under Peruvian consumer law, including filing a complaint with INDECOPI.

02

Your Rights

As a consumer of Grotix products and services, you are entitled to:

  • Receive a timely response to your submission within 30 calendar days.
  • Be notified of the outcome and any corrective actions taken.
  • Escalate unresolved complaints to INDECOPI (National Institute for the Defense of Competition and Intellectual Property) at any time.
  • Request a copy of your submission and any correspondence at no cost.

03

Resolution Process

Once your submission is received, Celevi will follow this process:

  • Acknowledgment — You will receive an email confirmation with a unique reference number within 2 business days.
  • Review — Our team will investigate the complaint or claim and may contact you for additional information.
  • Resolution — A formal response with our findings and proposed resolution will be sent within 30 calendar days of submission.
  • Follow-up — If you are unsatisfied with the resolution, you may escalate the matter to INDECOPI.

INDECOPI contact: www.indecopi.gob.pe — Phone: 224-7777 (Lima) / 0-800-4-4040 (free from landlines nationwide).
