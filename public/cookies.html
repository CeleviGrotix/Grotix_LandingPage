Cookies Policy

Grotix uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on its web platform and mobile application to ensure a reliable, personalized experience. This Policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

01

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use a web-based application. They allow the platform to remember information about your visit — such as your preferred language, authenticated session, or dashboard layout — so you don't have to reconfigure everything on each visit.

Similar technologies — such as local storage tokens and device identifiers used in our mobile app — serve the same functional purposes and are equally covered by this Policy.

Grotix does not use cookies for advertising, behavioral profiling, or cross-site tracking. Our use is strictly limited to what is necessary to operate the platform securely and effectively.

02

Types of Cookies We Use

We classify our cookies into four categories based on their purpose:

Type Purpose Required
Strictly Necessary Maintain your authenticated session (JWT token), enforce role-based access, and keep the platform secure. Without these, the service cannot function. Always active
Functional Remember your language preference (EN/ES), dashboard layout settings, and notification preferences so your configuration persists across sessions. Optional
Performance & Analytics Collect anonymized, aggregated data about how users interact with the platform — pages visited, features used, error rates — to help us improve reliability and usability. Optional
Security Detect and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access attempts, and CSRF attacks. These work alongside our JWT authentication layer. Always active

03

Third-Party Cookies

In limited cases, trusted third-party service providers used by Grotix may place their own cookies on your device. These providers are contractually bound to use such data only for the services they perform on our behalf and not for their own commercial purposes.

  • Cloud infrastructure providers — may set session-level cookies to route requests efficiently and maintain connection stability.
  • Analytics tools — if enabled, may use anonymized identifiers to compile aggregated usage statistics. No personally identifiable information is shared.
  • Email delivery services — may use tracking pixels in transactional emails (e.g., alert notifications) to confirm delivery. You can disable image loading in your email client to prevent this.

We do not integrate advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or any third-party cookie systems unrelated to platform operation.

04

Cookie Duration

Cookies used by Grotix fall into two duration categories:

  • Session cookies — exist only while your browser or app session is active. They are automatically deleted when you close the application or browser tab. Used primarily for authentication tokens and CSRF protection.
  • Persistent cookies — remain on your device for a defined period after your session ends. Our functional cookies (e.g., language preference) persist for up to 12 months, or until you clear them manually.

All cookie lifetimes are reviewed periodically. We aim to use the shortest retention period that still delivers the intended functionality.

05

Your Controls

You have several ways to manage or restrict cookies:

  • Platform preferences — you can toggle optional cookies (functional and analytics) from the Privacy Settings panel inside your Grotix account.
  • Browser settings — most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies at any time via their privacy or security settings. Refer to your browser's help documentation for specific instructions.
  • Mobile app — you can reset the app's local storage and revoke notification permissions from your device's application settings.
  • Opt-out of analytics — if analytics cookies are enabled, you may opt out at any time without affecting your ability to use the platform.

Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent the platform from functioning correctly, as they are essential for authentication and security.

06

Consent

When you first access the Grotix web platform, a cookie consent banner will inform you of the categories of cookies in use and request your permission for optional categories. Strictly necessary and security cookies are activated automatically as they are required for the service to operate.

Your consent choices are stored in your account settings and applied consistently across devices when you are logged in. You may withdraw or modify your consent at any time via the Privacy Settings panel.

Consent for optional cookies is never required to access core irrigation and telemetry features. Refusing optional cookies has no impact on sensor data collection, automated irrigation, or platform reliability.

07

Policy Changes

We may update this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in the technologies we use or in applicable regulations. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notification at least 15 days before the changes take effect. The updated policy will always be available on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this Policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies and what controls are available to you.
