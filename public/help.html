Getting started

Set up Grotix in 4 steps

1

Unbox and connect the hardware

Place the sensor node in your field, connect the power supply and attach the soil-moisture probe to the designated port.

2

Download the Grotix app

Available on Google Play and the App Store. Create your account or log in if you already have one.

3

Pair device via Wi-Fi

Open the app, tap "Add Device" and follow the on-screen instructions to connect your Grotix node to your local network.

4

Configure your crops and start monitoring

Point the camera at your plants for automatic AI identification, or choose your crop type manually. Grotix will handle the rest.
