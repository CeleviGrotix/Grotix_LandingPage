Privacy Policy

At Grotix, your privacy matters. This Policy explains what personal data we collect through our IoT hardware, mobile app, and web platform, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. We are committed to transparency and to complying with applicable data protection law.

01

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal information is Celevi S.A.C., the company behind the Grotix platform, incorporated under the laws of Peru and headquartered in Lima.

For all privacy-related inquiries, you may contact our Data Protection Officer at: privacy@grotix.io

02

Data We Collect

We collect data in three ways: information you provide directly, data generated automatically by our hardware, and data collected through your use of the app and website.

Category Examples Source
Account data Name, email address, password hash, preferred language Provided by user
Sensor telemetry Soil moisture, ambient temperature, relative humidity, sunlight levels, irrigation events IoT hardware
Crop data Crop type, phenological stage, manual configuration inputs Hardware / user input
Usage data App screens visited, feature interactions, session duration, error logs App & web platform
Device data Mobile OS version, device model, IP address, push token App & web platform
Communications Messages sent via contact form, support tickets Provided by user

Camera images captured by the machine-vision module for crop identification are processed entirely on-device. No raw images are transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

03

Purposes & Legal Basis

We process your data only for specific, legitimate purposes and always on a recognized legal basis under applicable data protection law.

  • Service delivery — to operate the irrigation platform, synchronize sensor data, and provide real-time telemetry (contractual necessity).
  • Account management — to authenticate users, manage roles, and secure access (contractual necessity).
  • AI model improvement — to refine irrigation algorithms and crop identification using anonymized, aggregated data (legitimate interest).
  • Push notifications & alerts — to inform you of sensor threshold events and irrigation activity (consent / contractual necessity).
  • Customer support — to respond to inquiries and resolve technical issues (legitimate interest).
  • Legal compliance — to comply with Peruvian data protection regulations and any applicable court orders (legal obligation).

We do not sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

04

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by law.

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your active account and deleted within 30 days of account closure upon request.
  • Sensor telemetry is retained for up to 24 months to support historical trend analysis and water-saving reports.
  • Anonymized, aggregated data derived from telemetry may be retained indefinitely for AI model training purposes.
  • Support communications are retained for 12 months after ticket resolution.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share it only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — cloud infrastructure partners (e.g., hosting, database) who process data strictly on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • Analytics providers — anonymized, aggregated usage metrics to improve the platform. No personally identifiable information is shared.
  • Legal requirements — when required by a valid court order, subpoena, or applicable law, we will disclose the minimum data necessary.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, who will be bound by this Policy.

06

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • TLS encryption for all data in transit between the hardware, app, and our servers.
  • JWT-based authentication and role-based access control (RBAC) for the platform.
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@grotix.io.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data. You can exercise most of these directly from your account settings panel, or by contacting us at privacy@grotix.io.

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction — ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interest, including for AI model improvement.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

We will respond to all valid requests within 30 days. If you are unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority in Peru (ANPD – Autoridad Nacional de Protección de Datos Personales).

08

Minors

The Grotix platform is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete the relevant information.

09

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make material changes, we will notify you via email or an in-app notification at least 15 days before the changes take effect. The updated policy will always be available on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10

Contact Our Data Protection Officer

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please reach out to our Data Protection Officer:

Email: privacy@grotix.io — We aim to respond within 5 business days. For complex or rights-related requests, our maximum response time is 30 calendar days.
