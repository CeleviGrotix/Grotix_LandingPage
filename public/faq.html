General

What is Grotix?

Grotix is an AI-driven precision irrigation platform developed by Celevi. It combines IoT sensors, machine vision and a mobile/web dashboard to help farmers optimize water use and improve crop health.

Who is Grotix designed for?

Grotix is designed for individual producers, smallholder farms and larger agricultural associations. The platform scales from a single sensor node to a multi-field network.

Where is Grotix available?

Grotix is currently available across Latin America, with priority support in Peru. We are expanding to further regions — contact us to check availability in your area.

Hardware

What does the Grotix sensor kit include?

Each kit includes a microcontroller node with integrated machine-vision module, a soil-moisture probe, an ambient temperature/humidity sensor, a sunlight sensor, a water pump controller and the necessary mounting hardware.

Does Grotix require constant internet connectivity?

Connectivity is needed to sync telemetry data to the dashboard and to use remote irrigation control. However, the on-device logic continues to operate autonomously during brief connectivity losses, buffering data until the connection is restored.

Can the device run on solar power?

Yes. Grotix sensor nodes support 5 V input via USB-C. A compatible solar panel with a battery management module can be used to power the device off-grid. Contact us for recommended third-party solar accessories.

Is the hardware weatherproof?

The enclosure is rated IP65, making it dustproof and protected against water jets. It is suitable for outdoor field deployment in most weather conditions.

App

Where can I download the Grotix app?

The app is available on Google Play for Android devices and the App Store for iOS. Search for "Grotix" or use the download buttons on our website.

Can I manage multiple fields from one account?

Yes. A single Grotix account can be linked to multiple sensor nodes and fields. You can switch between fields within the app and view each field's telemetry and irrigation logs independently.

Does the app send alerts or push notifications?

Yes. You can configure threshold-based alerts for soil moisture, ambient temperature and humidity. Push notifications are sent when values fall outside your defined ranges or when the pump activates.

Irrigation

How does automatic irrigation work?

The system continuously reads soil moisture, temperature, humidity and sunlight. When conditions indicate the plant needs water, the irrigation logic triggers the pump for a duration calculated from the crop's metabolic profile. No user action is required.

Can I water my crops manually from the app?

Yes. The app includes a manual irrigation button that triggers the pump immediately regardless of sensor conditions. You can also set a custom duration for the manual cycle.

How much water can Grotix save?

Results vary by crop and region, but early pilots showed water reduction of up to 35% compared to traditional fixed-schedule irrigation. The dashboard provides water-saving reports so you can track your own impact over time.

Privacy & Data

Where is my sensor data stored?

Sensor telemetry is encrypted in transit and stored on secure cloud servers. You retain ownership of your data and can export or delete it at any time from your account settings.

Are images from the crop camera shared with third parties?

No. Images are processed locally on the device for crop identification. Only aggregated, anonymized data may be used to improve model accuracy. No raw images are shared with third parties.

Billing

How much does Grotix cost?

Grotix is sold as a hardware kit with an optional subscription for advanced analytics, multi-field management and priority support. Contact us through the website for current pricing in your region.

What is your return and refund policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on hardware. If your device has a manufacturing defect or you are not satisfied within the first 30 days, contact us and we will arrange a return or replacement.
